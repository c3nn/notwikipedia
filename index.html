Can't cite wikipedia? Cite NOTwikipedia instead (if you want)

Many people argue the reliability of wikipedia and how "Anyone can edit it". I believe that wikipedia is (most of the time) a reliable source, and want the knowlage of wikipedia to be as accessible as posible. With this I developed NOTwikipedia, the website you cite when you can't cite wikipedia.
